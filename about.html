What is SchoolX?


A 9-year-old Syrian girl in Lebanon was unable to enroll in school. She decided to set up a small blackboard to teach the younger children in her refugee camp. Her story inspired us to make use of the abundant resource of refugee teachers and local volunteers.
Schoolx is an online platform that provides quality education to out-of-school children and youth worldwide. Using optimized algorithms, Schoolx oversees a robust educational system that connects trained local and refugee teachers to students, runs micro-classes and tracks students progress. 

What We Do

Certified Teacher Training

Any potential refugee teacher or local volunteer receives certified training.

Connecting Students to Teachers

Students are initially profiled. Our algorithms connect them to teachers to run micro-classes.

E-admin of Classes

Classes are app-organised and managed. Every student’s progress is tracked in real time.

How It Works


Sign Up

Download the app from App Store or Play Store. Sign up as a student or a teacher. Teachers and students’ parents/guardians can sign students up as well.

Set Your Profile

Let us know about yourself, your needs and interests. Choose which courses or entry year you would like to be enrolled.

Enrol in a Class

According to your academic profile and location, get connected to highly skilled, trained and certified teachers in the nearby. Join running classes or start a new one at your doorstep.

Connect and Track

Access supporting material, practice and communicate with your teacher. Parents can watch live stats of their children progress.

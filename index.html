About Us

Schoolx is inspired by a 9-year-old girl who was unable to enrol in school after arriving in Lebanon, set up a small blackboard under a tree and started teaching the younger children in her refugee camp what she remembered from her first-grade class in Syria.
Schoolx is a social enterprise that empowers the refugee community by training its teachers and connects them to students to run micro-classes within the refugee community, using tools of data science and mobile applications.

What We Do

Certified Teacher Training

Former teachers, unemployed parents, educated mothers, university students and dropouts or any candidate teachers and volunteers are trained online and offline on curricula, teaching methods and psychological support. Certificates will be given from one of our global partners.

Connecting Students to Teachers

Students are profiled according to age, origin, current location, personal history, previous education and entry level etc. Our innovative select and match algorithms will connect teachers to students in the most optimized way regarding subjects, time and place.

E-admin of Classes

Classes are app-organised and managed. Every student’s progress will be tracked and overseen by parents or guardians. This guarantees real time monitoring of the education process.

Flexible Pathway

Classes will be run in a flexible manner according to the teachers/student capacity and availability. The classes will prepare the students to sit for formal exams to qualify for governmental accreditation.

Data for Emergencies

The data collected will be a huge resource for international and governmental organizations to use for humanitarian action and emergency education.

How It Works

Sign Up

Download the app from App Store, Play Store or Win Store. Sign up as a student or a teacher.

Set Your Profile

Let us know about your interests, which courses you would like to take or you could teach, where you live etc.

Find Your Class

List the classes of your interest. If you are a student, you can join classes around you. If you are a teacher, you can open your own class (after a training/ approval?) and invite students to your class.

Go to SchoolX!

You can watch live sessions, download worksheets, do the practices and ask your questions to your teacher through the app. If you are a teacher, you can upload videos, worksheets, practice tests and you can always track the progress of your students.

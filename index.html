What is SchoolX

SchoolX is an online platform that facilitates the education process for out-of-school children in vulnerable areas.

What We Do

Certified Teacher Training

Any potential refugee teacher or local volunteer receives certified training.

Connecting Students to Teachers

Students are initially profiled. Our algorithms connect them to teachers to run micro-classes.

E-admin of Classes

Classes are app-organised and managed. Every student’s progress is tracked in real time.

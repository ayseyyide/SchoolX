About Us


Schoolx is a global online platform that provides quality education to out-of-school children and youth worldwide. Using optimized algorithms, Schoolx oversees a robust educational system that connects trained local and refugee teachers to students, runs micro-classes and tracks students progress.

A 9-year-old Syrian girl in Lebanon who was unable to enroll in school decided to set up a small blackboard to teach the younger children in her refugee camp. Her story inspired SchoolX to make use of and empower the untapped resource of refugee teachers.

What We Do

Certified Teacher Training

Any potential refugee teachers such as educated parents, university students, as well as local volunteers receive online and offline training. Certification is given by one of our global partners.

Connecting Students to Teachers

Students are profiled according to important criteria such as location and educational history. Our innovative algorithms connect students to teachers in a way that best suits their needs.

E-admin of Classes

Classes are app-organised and managed. Every student’s progress is tracked and overseen by parents or guardians. This guarantees real time monitoring of the educational process.

Flexible Pathway

Classes are run in a flexible manner according to teacher/student capacity and availability i.e. anytime-anywhere. The classes prepare the students to sit formal, governmentally accredited exams.

Data for Emergencies

The data collected is a huge resource for international and governmental organizations to be used for humanitarian action and emergency education.

Free of Charge

NGO funding and donations means that classes are free of charge for the students. Funding covers teacher’s salaries who in most cases are refugees, thus economically empowering the refugee community.

How It Works


Sign Up

Download the app from App Store or Play Store. Sign up as a student or a teacher. Teachers and students’ parents/guardians can sign students up as well.

Set Your Profile

Let us know about yourself, your needs and interests. Choose which courses or entry year you would like to be enrolled.

Enrol in a Class

According to your academic profile and location, get connected to highly skilled, trained and certified teachers in the nearby. Join running classes or start a new one at your doorstep.

Connect and Track

Access supporting material, practice and communicate with your teacher. Parents can watch live stats of their children progress.
